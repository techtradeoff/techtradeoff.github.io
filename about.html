What I do

I design high-scale, distributed platforms for enterprise organisations — translating complex business objectives into resilient technical strategies that engineering teams can actually execute. Most of my work sits at the intersection of architecture, platform engineering, and agentic AI.

On the commercial side, I've run technical presales on large enterprise deals: solutioning, discovery, architecture defence, and the structured thinking that turns complex requirements into a win.

Why TechTradeoff

After spending years in senior roles, I kept running into the same gap: senior-level problems with no senior-level tools. LLMs that gave beginner answers. Playbooks written for teams of 10. Documentation patterns that didn't survive real scrutiny.

TechTradeoff started as a newsletter — deconstructing the hardest tradeoffs in enterprise architecture, distributed systems, and technical leadership. The open source tools came from that same place: things I built because I needed them, and made public because others do too.
